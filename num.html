Introduction

                              Counting things is easy for us now. We can count objects in large numbers, for example, the number of students in the school, and represent them through numerals. We can also communicate large numbers using suitable number names.

                                       It is not as if we always knew how to convey large quantities in conversation or through symbols. Many thousands years ago, people knew only small numbers. Gradually, they learnt how to handle larger numbers. They also learnt how to express large numbers in symbols. All this came through collective efforts of human beings. Their path was not easy, they struggled all along the way. In fact, the development of whole of Mathematics can be understood this way. As human beings progressed, there was greater need for development of Mathematics and as a result Mathematics grew further and faster.

                                       We use numbers and know many things about them. Numbers help us count concrete objects. They help us to say which collection of objects is bigger and arrange them in order e.g., first, second, etc. Numbers are used in many different contexts and in many ways. Comparing numbers

Let us see how to find out the largest and smallest numbers among given list of numbers.
Steps to be followed:

1.Find out the number of digits in each number and compare those.The number which has more number of digits is the greatest and which has least number of digits is the smallest.

2.If the numbers have same number of digits then compare the digits in left most place of the number.For example,if the number has 4 digits in it,then compare the thousands place of all the numbers.The number which has greatest number in its left most place is the large number and quite opposite goes with smallest number.

3.Now,if the digit in first left most place is also the same, then go on comparing digits from left to right.

This is the way to figure out the largest and smallest numbers among given list of numbers.

Example:

a.Find out the largest and smallest numbers among 92, 392, 4456, 89742.
ans:No. of digits in 92 - 2
      No. of digits in 392 - 3
      No. of digits in 4456 - 4
      No. of digits in 89742 - 5
           Therefore,the largest number is 89742 and smallest is 92.

b.Find out the largest and smallest numbers among 75,489,3484,3472.
ans:No. of digits in 75 - 2
      No. of digits in 489 - 3
      No. of digits in 3484 - 4
      No. of digits in 3472 - 4
           Here,we have two numbers with same number of digits.So,compare the digits from left to right. Both the numbers 3484 and       3472 have same numbers in their thousands place and hundreds place. The digits in tens place in numbers 3484 and 3472 are       8 and 7 respectively.Therefore,3484 is the largest and 75 is the smallest among given four numbers.

How many numbers can you make?

Suppose, we have four digits 7, 8, 3, 5. Using these digits we want to make different 4-digit numbers such that no digits are repeated in a number. Thus, 7835 is allowed, but 7735 is not. Make as many 4-digit numbers as you can.
       Which is the greatest number you can get? Which is the smallest number?
              The greatest number is 8753 and the smallest is 3578.

Ascending order: Ascending order means arrangement from the smallest to the greatest. [23,345,5678,6789]
Descending order: Descending order means arrangement from the greatest to the smallest.[6789,5678,345,23]
